Tenants Portal

We use a management system that allows for complete transparency:

Tenant Portal- As long as you have a smart device (phone, computer & internet) you can see up to date information, tenant leases, property inspections and property condition reports instantly.

  • Apply for properties electronically and upload required documents.
  • Access, view & sign electronic acceptance of lease
  • View all PRC with photos, which allows you to make comments, changes and take photos electronically through our portal.
  • Electronic email reminder and notifications for rent arrears, property inspections, maintenance requests etc
  • View current lease and rent information
  • Automatic rental ledger viewing 
  • Communicate directly with your property manager
  • Transparency for entry and exit property condition reports so you can get your bond back in full.
