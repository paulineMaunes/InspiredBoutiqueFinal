About Us




Let Us Inspire you at Inspired Boutique Property!
Inspired Boutique Property is a boutique property agency, offering you an exclusive property difference.

We offer exclusive end to end property services:
- Property Management
- Buyer’s Agent
- Selling Agent
- Property Consultancy
- Studio & Cabins

Property Management - Relationships
We are a small exclusive and personalised boutique agency, which is our point of difference. We build great long-lasting relationships with you and your tenants to ensure long tenancies. You engage us as your property manager and we ‘manage’ your tenants according, through respectful fair, firm & consistent interactions. Our tenants understand we have a zero tolerance on late rent & property damage/maintenance and act accordingly. As a result, we have a lot of tenants who have been with us for a minimum of 2 years – 5 years. I guess they love us! Happy tenants equal happy owners (and agents). We have a history of gaining good rental yields for our clients through out consultancy service. We touch base with our owners regularly, just to say hi and have a ‘good news’ call. We would love for you to consider our service and come and enjoy what it means to have an exclusive Inspired Boutique experience.

Buyer’s Agent - How can we help you! 
When you engage us as your Buyers Agent, we act exclusively for you!
We take the stress and worry out of finding the right investment property of you, saving you time and money whether you are a first time investor, a seasoned investor or buying your for-ever home, we have you covered.
Our difference is that we listen to you! We listen to what you want and we diligently carry out your needs with our professional expertise providing you with a personal tailored plan, that meets your individual needs.
We can look after you through the whole buying process from purchasing the right property on (or off-market) all the way through to property management. We provide you with detailed market analysis, so you feel protected and informed about your choices.
Come and enjoy the exclusive boutique difference!

Sale - Exclusive and personalised.  
We are a small exclusive and personalised boutique agency, which is our point of difference. We build great long-lasting relationships with you, that exceeds your original purchase. We have access to potential buyers and excellent marketing strategies to assist you sell your property efficiently.

Consultancy – Proven knowledge and experience.
Our consultancy service can offer you an exclusive range of proven strategies to assist you and educate you in gaining the maximum potential from your investment. Whether it’s increasing the rental yield by $100pw, purchasing the right property and adding value to your portfolio, selling your property quicker or for a better price, or finding a profitable way through divorce and your family’s biggest asset. Whilst we are not a financial consultant, we have proven knowledge and experience to support you to get the most out of your property and property investment. Or if you have a project that you would like assistance with we can offer a project management service. As smooth well timed project saves time and money. With our extensive list of exceptional contractors that we have trialled before, we know we can get your job done efficiently and to a high standard.
Let us help you!
